Welcome to Melodia Event Management Company. We are an ISO 9001:2015-certified event management company in Kerala with over 15+ years of experience in the field. Melodia Event Management has successfully planned over 550 weddings, 70 corporate events, 20 inaugurations, and 150 other private events over the past decade, up to 2025. What started as a small firm has now expanded into a multi-locality event management service in Kerala. Our hard work has enabled us to step out of the neighborhoods of Thrissur to the popular territories of Calicut and Cochin.
